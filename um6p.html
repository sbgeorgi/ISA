UM6P Agrivoltaics Timeline

2022

Research on Luminescent Solar Concentrators

UM6P researchers proposed using luminescent solar concentrators (LSCs) in agrivoltaic installations to convert UV light to visible light for crop growth and generate electricity, enhancing system efficiency.

2023

Strengthening Partnership with Fauji Group

UM6P and OCP Group reinforced partnership with Pakistan's Fauji Group, with UM6P contributing agronomic expertise to promote innovative agricultural solutions, potentially including agrivoltaics.

2023

Establishment of Agrivoltaic Research Platform

UM6P created a replicated agrivoltaic platform at its Benguerir experimental farm to study energy and crop co-production in semi-arid conditions, focusing on scalability for Morocco and Sub-Saharan Africa.

2024

Focus Group on Sustainable Agri-Food Policies

UM6P's College of Agriculture organized a focus group to identify policies for promoting WEFE Nexus opportunities in the Rhamna region, including hydroponics with photovoltaics.

2024

Wiser Cares Adaptive Agriculture Visit to UM6P

UM6P hosted Wiser Cares Adaptive Agriculture team, showcasing agrivoltaic work and emphasizing the dual benefits of agrivoltaic panels and the importance of water management in MENA region.

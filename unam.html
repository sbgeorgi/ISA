UNAM Agrivoltaics Timeline

2023

Establishment of Mexico's First Agrivoltaic Plot at UNAM

UNAM inaugurated the country's first agrivoltaic installation, known as the Sustainable and Educational Agrivoltaic Plot (PASE). Located at CEPIPSA, it aimed to enhance agricultural productivity, generate green energy, reduce water consumption, and disseminate new technologies.

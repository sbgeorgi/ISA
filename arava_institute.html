Arava Institute Agrivoltaics Timeline

2021

Early Initiatives and Research

The Arava Institute established an agrivoltaics research site in collaboration between the Center for Renewable Energy and Energy Conservation and the Center for Sustainable Agriculture. Research focuses on dual land use for agriculture and energy, crop yield enhancement, and water efficiency.

2022

Public Outreach and Knowledge Sharing

Dr. Tali Zohar from the Arava Institute conducted public outreach through presentations and webinars. These sessions aimed to share insights on the benefits and challenges of agrivoltaics and promote sustainable land-use practices among stakeholders.

2023

Community Engagement and Education

Interns at the Arava Institute engaged in agrivoltaics research, focusing on community-based microgrids, flywheel storage, and desalination for off-grid areas. These projects highlight practical agrivoltaic applications for energy and water solutions.

Read more
2024

Technological Advancements - SPARC

Development of the Solar-Powered Agri Robotic Classroom (SPARC), an autonomous agricultural robot for seeding, watering, harvesting, and crop monitoring under solar panels. SPARC enhances farming efficiency and serves as an educational tool for sustainable agriculture.
